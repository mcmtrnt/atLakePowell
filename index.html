Stay at Lake Powell

Nestled near Page, AZ, our Airbnb offers a perfect blend of comfort and adventure, just moments away from the stunning Lone Rock Beach and popular Wahweap Marina.

  • Spacious accomodations
  • Sleeps 10 (6 beds, 1 pullout couch)
  • 3 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms
  • Prime Location
  • 5 min to Lone Rock Beach
  • 7 min to Waweap Marina

Our Vacation Home is located in a quiet, beautiful, peaceful community on the Utah/ Arizona border. It is the perfect place to relax and enjoy beautiful Lake Powell with friends and family.

Access to Lone Rock and Wahweap Beach are within minutes.

Enjoy the stunning resort quality views of Lake Powell while paddle boarding, kayaking, boating and just relaxing on the beach.

Everything you'll need for a comfortable stay is here, including the fastest wireless internet access in the entire area, central heating and air conditioning, laundry room, a gas BBQ and a large 55" flat screen TV, one of 3 flat screen TVs that come equipped with Netflix and Amazon Prime access - sure to please the whole family's entertainment needs.

To add to the home's well-equipped interior, the living area has new reclining leather sofas and beautiful furniture. The spacious living area will quickly become your favorite place to relax after a long day of outdoor exploration and lake fun, featuring high ceilings and nice open windows framing panoramic views of Lake Powell's shimmering waters. The large dining table provides room to enjoy games and family meals with your loved ones as the aroma of a delicious home cooked meal radiates from the open kitchen, which is complete with additional bar seating and all new appliances you'll need to cook the best home cooked meals.

After eating dinner at the large dining table, head outside for a tranquil evening of storytelling and laughter on the porch, then enjoy the calmness of the area and dark skies as you gaze at the amazing sky.

Afterwards, head inside for a night of restful sleep in the nice “new” beds offered in each of the three bedrooms, to re-energize for another exciting day in beautiful Lake Powell.

Despite incredible comfort at the home and astounding views of the mesas and lake, be sure to explore the surrounding area to experience all the excitement the area has to offer. For the outdoor enthusiasts in your group, Antelope Canyon, Horseshoe Bend, Glen Canyon Damn, and Rainbow Bridge are some of the many incredible places to visit the beautiful views of the area.

Lake Powell area has many restaurants nearby for an evening of local culture and dining. Regardless of what time of year it is or how you choose to spend your time here, this exquisite Lake Powell vacation home will provide you with everything you'll need for an unforgettable getaway!
